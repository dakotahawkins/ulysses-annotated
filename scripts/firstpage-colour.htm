This is the annotated edition of Ulysses, by James Joyce.

The body of the novel is derived from the Project Gutenberg eBook of Ulysses, the source files for which are available here, under the terms of the Gutenberg License, which can be found here.

The annotations in this ebook are excerpts automatically generated from longer notes on joyceproject.com with the permission of the site's creator and curator, John Hunt. Under the terms of a Creative Commons license, his work may be freely copied and distributed if three conditions are met: no commercial uses, no alterations, and full attribution.

The ebook is updated every Sunday with the latest notes from the website. You can download the latest version of the ebook from http://joyceproject.com, and older versions from github.

This is the colour version, where notes numbers are colour coded based on the type of link. There is also a monochrome version, if that works better for you.
